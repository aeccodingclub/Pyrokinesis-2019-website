Responsibilities

What are your responsibilities

Pyrokinesis, Assam Engineering College is one of the largest college fests in North-East India. Embodying the true spirit of youth, Pyrokinesis provides a platform for young talent to showcase their varied talents.
* Increase the reach of Pyrokinesis in your college.
* Online publicity of Pyrokinesis through social media in your college.
* Assist in getting registrations from your college for various events taking place in the fest.
* Merchandise Pyrokinesis Official T-shirt in your college.
* Provide ideas and suggestions to make the fest better.
