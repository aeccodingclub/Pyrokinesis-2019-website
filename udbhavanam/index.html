About Udbhavanam 7.0

The Annual Technical festival , aptly named Udbhavanam caters to the buzzing engineering students to play their knowledge to know , build or create something worthwhile with significant contribution towards to society. It boasts of different workshops , events and competition to bring forward the knowledge and application of it towards fruitful goals. Udbhavanam runs parallel to Pyrokinesis.

Events

Code War

Codewar is the Annual Competitive Programming event organised by the Coding Club. It is where developers achieve code mastery through challenges.

R-AEC 2.0

The ROBO CLUB AEC presents you the second edition of R-AEC with a series of exciting events in a more bigger and better way this time under Udbhavanam 7.0.

Headshot 4.0

Headshot is a best-in-class enthusiastic sport event. It involves tests of accuracy, precision and speed in shooting. In this event, the participants will be able to gain knowledge about various types of shooting guns.

Constellate

Constellate" comprises of a workshop cum miniature electrical vehicle prototyping competition followed by racing of hence designed prototypes

Build Xtra

Making bridges with popsicle sticks. Each will consists of maximum 3 members and they will be provided sticks ,thread and glue to build.

Chem Quiz

Annual chemistry quiz of AEC which has only grown bigger every year. Test out your love for chemistry trivia or improve it even more by attending it this year.

Aero Tech

A workshop on Aerofoil design and building of RC aircraft / glider for a duration of two days. An optional session on Quad-rotor anatomy on the final day of the workshop.
