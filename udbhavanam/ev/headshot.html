Introduction

Headshot is a best-in-class enthusiastic sport event. It involves tests of accuracy, precision and speed in shooting. In this event, the participants will be able to gain knowledge about various types of shooting guns. They will learn how to use them in the training session which will be helpful in self-defence. The candidate will be trained under the supervision of experts in this respective field. Headshot is a two days event which will be held in Nehru Stadium shooting range. The candidates will be taken there by the transportation facilities provided by us. After reaching the place and before participation, the experts will teach them about different types of guns, how to hold and shoot. Also they will be allowed to practice under their guidance before actually taking part in the competition.
