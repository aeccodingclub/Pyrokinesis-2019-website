Aerotech

A workshop on Aerofoil design and building of RC aircraft / glider for a duration of two days. An optional session on Quad-rotor anatomy on the final day of the workshop.

Introduction

Aerotech is a workshop dedicated to Aerofoil design and building of a Remote Controlled Plane / Glider from scratch. With the increase in demand for Unmanned Arial Vehicle (UAV) throughout the world, the need for research in this field is at its peak. This workshop aims to inculcate the basics of UAV starting from the Aerodynamics involved, the design of the model (aerofoil) to building of your very own RC plane / glider.
The workshop will be held in the college premises with two sessions, the first session will be an indoor session involving the build and the various teaching sessions, and the second will an outdoor session involving the testing and flying of their very own RC plane / glider under the supervision of the instructor. The participants will also learn about the various electrical and mechanical components involved in the build and how tinkering with each components will change the attributes of the UAV.
The workshop itself will promote hands on experience which should increase the practical knowledge of the participants as well as develop a habit of working and sharing within a group.
An optional session on Quadcopter Anatomy and practical demonstration will take place after the flying session which should promote curiosity and inspire people to look into this amazing new field with unlimited application and possibilities.
