R-AEC 2.0

The ROBO CLUB AEC presents you the second edition of R-AEC with a series of exciting events in a more bigger and better way this time under Udbhavanam 7.0.

Introduction

Starting off with the ROBOWAR, a grueling combat where competitors need to bring their robots ready to battle against each other in the arena with an aim to kick the opponents robot out of the arena. The arena will be without hurdles or water bodies and will be conducted in two rounds i.e. the semifinals followed by the finals. ROBOWAR will be then followed by the ROBOSOCCER on the same day which is a grueling combat between robots where the main aim is to play soccer and win maximum goals! The arena will be without hurdles or water bodies, to be conducted again in three rounds, thus eventually marking the end of the first day of R-AEC. The second day of R-AEC brings along the newer edition under the banner of R-AEC –Techville, a technical model presentation cum exhibition with the theme-“To build the engineer inside you” and targeting the school-going students and the junior semesters i.e. upto 3rd year of under graduation. The competition will be conducted in two categories keeping in view their exposure and class of study.
