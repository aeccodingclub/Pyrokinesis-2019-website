Chem Quiz

Annual chemistry quiz of AEC which has only grown bigger every year. Test out your love for chemistry trivia or improve it even more by attending it this year.

Introduction

CHEM QUIZ is the annual chemistry quiz under Udbhawanam. Currently in it's 7th edition, it aims to test the participants knowledge about various chemistry topics and trivia. Furthermore it also aims to enlighten and enrich these skills of all the people attending the event in a fun yet competitive format. The event will be conducted by Mr Kapinjal Chowdhury, renowned quizmaster and esteemed alumni of AEC. The event will be at the Seminar Hall, Chemical Building and quizzers will have to battle it out through two compelling rounds to claim the title of this year's winners.
