Build Xtra

The tech fest Udbhavanam is back with another installment of the Build-xtra, 2019 and this time its gonna get better. Here you create your own bridge, enhance it for better, and let it sustain the kg's out there!

Introduction

"Until you cross your bridge of your insecurities, you can't begin to explore your possibilities." Exactly!! So guys, here we are back again with our extremely exciting and challenging event 'Build-Xtra' 2019 under the Tech-fest Udbhabanam. As the name says, the event is primarily based on designing your own bridge (yessss !!) , enhance it for better, and let it sustain the barrier out there!! (you see, my bridge can sustain 10 kilos! Ohh, mine upto 18!) Come play, check your creativity. Fun staff over, let's get serious in here. Although Build-xtra primarily focuses on the realms of Structural Design and Civil Engineering, the analysis and judgements will be based on much that of your creativity and talent. So, it is not important for you to be a Civil Engineer to participate. The event will instead provide you the opportunity to harbor your talents, develop creativity and to indulge in you the knowledge of what causes failure in structures, how bridges are constructed and how to add a boost to your CVs. So guys, what to worry about? Buckle up, make a team of maximum 3, and show off your talent at this popsicle bridge designing competition
