Constellate

" Constellate" comprises of a workshop cum miniature electrical vehicle prototyping competition followed by racing of hence designed prototypes

Introduction

As we are gearing up for the most awaited technical event of the year, " Udbhavanam", the Automobile Club gladly presents "Constellate". Constellate invites youngsters from different nook and corner of the city with a passion of learning. The event marks a workshop cum prototyping competition where each team of maximum TWO members will build a prototype of miniature electrical vehicle. The designed prototypes will then be put to test in a tournament style racing competition to claim the cash prize. The teams shall be judged on the time required for building the prototype, durability ,speed and time of completion of race. Maximum Teams allowed - 40.
