        Social        Initiatives



        SOVIC, the social responsibility cell of AEC has piled up events this year too in social interest. From organizing Fundraiser Events to visiting Orphanages to old age home, it provides ample outlook to the people volunteering around them.
        Established on 28thJune 2018. SOVIC had been operating in all the three districts of Barak Valley. The sole motive is to live up to the hopes of the orphaned, needy and the darker sections of the society. It has taken up stand in issues where people tend to shy away. It's a student body volunteering and working up to a society's consciousness regarding its betterment and general welfare.

PRAYASH

This year SOVIC alongside PYROKINESIS have decided to visit PARIJAT Academy and NEET and spend quality time during the weekend with the children, several literary events are also to be held alongside various fun events. A day before Pyrokinesis an event is scheduled to visit Old Age Home named Monalisa Society.

CHILDREN FEST

Then fundraiser event has been scheduled during the 4 days scheduled for the main events alongside a event of CHILDREN FEST on 20th morning. The evenings are reserved for the fundraising events which will include games and other entertainment to engage the crowd.
